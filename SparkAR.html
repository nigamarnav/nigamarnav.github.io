Problem Brief
Develop a creative learning effect to facilitate understanding of school students on: Mathematics - Basic Arithmetic (addition/substraction/multiplication/division)

Brainstorming
‍Children often find themselves difficulty in understanding fractions. To solve this problem, we worked on the idea of showing basic mathematical operations of fractions in Augmented reality.
