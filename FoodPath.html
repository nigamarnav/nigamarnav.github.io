Zaika


Self-Initiated Project (Feb - March 2021)

Brief:

Zaika is an app for travelers, intended to help them in finding restaurants while promoting local food and taste.

Tools : Figma, Google Forms, Tableau


Problem Statement

How might we help people in finding a restaurant with local food and taste while traveling to a new place?

User Research

I started user research by talking to the people directly about their experience of going to a restaurant while traveling to a new place. It was observed that there were three different user-groups -

A. Youngsters : Explorer/Mostly students, freshers
B. Middle Aged : Quality Time spender/Mostly couples, families
C. Old Aged : Experienced/Mostly elder people in families

These user-groups were very different and non-overlapping in their needs and experience. I narrowed down our user-group to Youngsters only because they are the ones who were keen to explore new things online more. For detailed research, I conducted a survey, in which 42 people aged 20â€“30 participated.

User Interview and Personas

Based on the inputs gained from survey, I defined my target user-group as youngsters whose purpose of traveling is to visit and have fun. I spoke to 6 people around these questions about their travel experience -


  1. How they chose restaurants while traveling?
  2. Where they searched? Online/Asking people?
  3. How much google search or map helped?
  4. How did they go to the restaurant?
  5. What about lunch? What about dinner?

Based on the experience and requirements of the users, I created two personas to represent target users.
