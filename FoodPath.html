Brief:
FoodPath helps in finding best resturant while travelling to a new place.

User Research

We started our research by talking to the people directly on their experience of going to a resturant while travelling to a new-place. We found that there were three different user-groups -
A. Youngsters : Explorer/Mostly students, freshers
A. Middle Aged : Quality Time spender/Mostly couples, families
A. Old Aged : Experienced/Mostly elder people in families

These user-groups were very distinct in their needs and experience. Therefore, We narrowed down our user-group to Youngsters. For a detailed research we did a survey, where 42 people between the age-group of 20-30yrs participated.
