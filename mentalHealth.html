Worry Not

OVERVIEW

Worry Not is a mobile application that can be used by mental health patients to take advice from the Psychiatrists based on their health-condition.

This was created for the challenge given by IDC IIT Bombay on the theme 'D'source Corona Design challenge'.
May 2020
Behavior is contagious. Insanity is contagious.

— Psychiatry of Pandemics

Background

This pandemic has challenged our entire structure of society and pushed us to accept a “New Normal”. Coronavirus is changing not only our external world but also our inner within. Several reports and news articles have been published that say how the number of mental health patients got increased in this period. So I went a little bit ahead and did research on the mental health impact of COVID 19 and lined out a novel approach to face the problem.
Worry Not won D'source Corona Design Award.
This was an amazing experience. Some key achievements listed below:
  • Learning Design Process and Thinking. This project has taught me how to effectively apply design process to solve the problem.
  • Psychology is backbone of Design. Psychology provides evidence for design decision. It provides multiple view-point.
  • Happy starting. First project where I found to be immersed in Design. Beginning in UX with my first Design Award.

Understanding the problem

To understand the problem, I went through news-articles, surveys done by various organization and social media profile of those who were badly affected by COVID-19. Some of my key findings were:
  • PTSD has been found in many cases for longer duration of quarantine.
  • Fear of infection and stigmatization of pandemic makes it worse.
  • Boredom and bad communication lead to frustration.
  • People are seeking many types of information from many different sources.

Problem Statement

How might we can help people who are going through bad mental state in this unseen situation?

Initial Findings

Based on the initial research, I did affinity mapping to identify:

  • Vulnerable population : People, who are at the edge of mental health conditions in the time of crisis.
  • Changes : What major changes have occurred due to COVID-19 pandemic.
  • Mental Health Triggers : Common triggers that are behind worse conditions.
  • Cultural and Social Aspect : How people handle the situation, cultural and social values defines a major role here.

Psychological Understanding of Pandemics

To give my research an orientation, I looked upon a few of the psychological theories that explain what people face in this situation and how it affects their mental health.
  • Zombie Fear: Common to the generally held beliefs about both zombies and mental illness is the idea of being taken over and losing one’s self-determination by intentionally avoiding logical thinking. “Insanity is contagious.”
  • Behavioral Contagion Theory: “spontaneous, unsolicited and uncritical imitation of another’s behavior” when people are sharing similar situation in panic.
  • Transtheoretical Model:People are seeing how trusted figures are publicly supporting changes, feeling inspired by shared stories and are getting emotional and practical support from their families and friends.

User Research and Personas

User personas based on Coronavirus patients stories and interview

Insights

Key-Challenges

  • Information intake and news addiction is increasing.
  • Religious significance and various interpretations of religious texts is playing major role in stigmas.
  • Faith in doctors make people feel safe and secure.
  • Number of psychiatrists is less as compared to number of patients.
  • Need of minute to minute monitor.
  • Most of the existing apps doesn’t provide real time feedback.

Designs

Simple design with the motivation to collect information that are needed by Psychiatrists to determine disorders, and, then giving patients personalized advice.

 Users-details to better understand the situation
Monitoring Physical and Mental Health
Users can put their daily physical and mental well-being.
Sleep Cycle

 Page where Psychiatrist can give their advice.

Contact
Contact your doctor. Whatsapp or Call.

How the app is useful

  • Daily Monitoring and Personal Suggestions
  • Better Understanding of patients
  • Measuring News Addiction
  • Contact doctor in emergency.
  • All the data is categorical.
  • Solving issue of less number of doctors

    • (Using previous property, patients having similar symptoms can be grouped together (data-visualization and clustering) and treated together by the doctors.)
