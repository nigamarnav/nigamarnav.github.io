Virtubox - No Code Platform for App, Web, and Kiosk


UI/UX Design Internship (August 2020 - Present)

About the Company:
Virtubox is a No-Code Platform for creating, sharing, and deploying interactive content for three mediums - App, Website, and Kiosk. It offers cloud-based solutions on the Saas Model for various types of businesses.

Team:
Arnav (Myself) - UI/UX Designer
Jyoti - Product Manager

Work:
My role is to build a prototype of the No-Code platform that can be delivered to the Development Team for implementation. The prototype should fulfill the business and user-requirements while providing a delightful user experience.
In simple terms, my work involves -
1. User Research and Usability Testing
2. Market Research
3. UI Design - Wireframing and Prototyping
