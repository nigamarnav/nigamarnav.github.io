The Problem

Little kids who don't know how to write letters and alphabets, needs hand in hand help. Volunteers at Jagrati provide them tracing-activity sheets and other supports. Also, we often lack in number of volunteers and supporting materials. Some of the problems with tracing-activity sheets are listed below:
  • Children find tracing over dotted lines to be an uninteresting exercise.
  • It promotes rote memorization and passive learning.
  • We noticed that children were practicing letter-writing for the whole year and learning very less from it.

This situation is almost every place where teachers have limited amount of time and student-teacher ratio is very high.


Need and Seed

In fast changing world, Education needs a shift from...

  • Rote Memorization to Creative and Interactive Learning.
  • Passive to Active Learning.
  • Mere Reading to Visual Perception.

Almost every home in the village, had a smartphone, where we visited. And, the seed of solution is in effective use of available technology. See the video.
